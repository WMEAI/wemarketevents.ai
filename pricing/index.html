Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a minimum commitment?
No. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime. Annual plans offer a 20% discount and are billed upfront for the year. We recommend annual for multi-event organisers — the savings add up fast.
What is Lights On?
Lights On is an optional post-event add-on for $150/month (2 months, $300 upfront). It keeps your dashboards live and Lens reporting active after your event ends — so you can deliver final stakeholder reports, run post-event analysis, and preserve benchmarks for your next cycle. No auto-renewal.
What data sources do you connect to?
All plans include connectors for GA4, Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads, Meta Ads, major email platforms, and registration systems. The Command tier adds custom connector setup for non-standard sources, CRM integrations (HubSpot, Salesforce), and enrichment platforms.
How quickly can I get started?
Most clients are live within 48 hours. We handle connector setup, benchmark calibration against your event type, and initial dashboard configuration during onboarding. You'll see your first Lens intelligence brief within 24 hours of data flowing.
What makes Lens different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?
Lens is purpose-built for event marketing, trained on patterns from real B2B event campaigns across every major vertical. It doesn't just summarise data — it detects performance shifts in real time, diagnoses root causes with evidence, forecasts outcomes with confidence bands, and recommends specific actions with expected impact. Generic AI tools can't do any of that.
Can I upgrade mid-engagement?
Yes. You can upgrade from EventPulse + Lens to Command at any time. You'll pay the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing period.
Do I need technical expertise to use EventPulse?
No. EventPulse is designed for marketing leads and event managers, not data engineers. The Copilot lets you ask questions in plain English. Executive Narratives are auto-generated in CMO-friendly language. If you can read a report, you can use EventPulse.
What's the benchmark database based on?
Our benchmark intelligence comes from years of B2B event campaigns spanning trade shows, conferences, exhibitions, and summits across every major vertical. Every metric — CPA, conversion rate, funnel velocity, creative fatigue — is contextualised against events of similar type, size, and industry.
